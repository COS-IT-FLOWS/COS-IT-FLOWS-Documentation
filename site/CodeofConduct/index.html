CODE OF CONDUCT

  1. Inclusivity and Diversity

    • Welcome contributions from individuals of all backgrounds and identities.
    • Foster a culture of respect, empathy, and understanding.

  2. Openness and Transparency

    • Conduct all discussions and decision-making processes in an open and transparent manner.
    • Share information, resources, and knowledge openly with the community.

  3. Respect for Contributors

    • Respect the time, effort, and expertise of all contributors.
    • Provide constructive feedback and support to help contributors improve.

  4. Collaboration and Cooperation

    • Encourage collaboration and cooperation among community members.
    • Support initiatives that benefit the broader open-source community.

  5. Accountability

    • Hold all community members accountable for their actions and contributions.
    • Take responsibility for addressing and resolving conflicts or issues within the community.

  6. Ethical Behavior

    • Conduct all activities in accordance with ethical standards and legal requirements.
    • Respect intellectual property rights and licensing agreements.

  7. Professionalism

    • Maintain a professional and courteous demeanor in all interactions.
    • Represent the community in a positive and respectful manner.

  8. Continuous Learning and Improvement

    • Encourage continuous learning and skill development among community members.
    • Strive for continuous improvement in processes, tools, and community.
